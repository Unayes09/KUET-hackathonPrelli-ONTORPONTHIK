
      Title: Spaghetti Carbonara
      Ingredients: 200g spaghetti, 100g pancetta, 2 large eggs, 50g pecorino cheese, 50g parmesan cheese, Freshly ground black pepper, Salt, 1 clove of garlic, A small bunch of fresh flat-leaf parsley
      Instructions: 1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. 2. Meanwhile, cut the pancetta into small cubes and fry in a pan with the garlic until crispy. 3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then finely grate in the pecorino and parmesan cheese. Mix well. 4. Remove the garlic from the pancetta pan and discard. 5. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some of the cooking water. 6. Add the spaghetti to the pancetta pan and remove from heat. 7. Quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring vigorously to coat the pasta. Add a little reserved pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce. 8. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper. 9. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley.
      Taste: Rich and creamy
      Reviews: 4.7
      Cuisine Type: Italian
      Preparation Time: 20 minutes
